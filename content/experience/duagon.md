### Technical Sales Manager for tailored Electronics Solutions

After many experiences in the technical world, I started to explore the business aspects of engineered products within [Knorr-Bremse Duagon](https://www.duagon.com/). 🎛️

Applications in the railway sector, in which the customer base is large and multi-colored, must be safe and with high-reliability: this means strict compliancy with norms and products whose lifecycle spans over 20+ years. 🚋
On top of that, several government-driven norms like the European Cyber Resilience Act (CRA) pose additional challenges in the development and marketability of electronics products.

Customers demand effective and complex solutions in short times, then the interface with them has to understand technical requirements and commercial aspects in a short time-frame.
This is where a ***techical-commercial figure*** like mine plays a significative role in selling tailored solutions which bring business value. 📊

Given the astoundishing improvement of the Artificial Intelligence in the past years, I actively try improving my workflow by testing new AI-technologies like RAG and Agents. 🧠

Results:
* Established market-relationship with accounts which bring the 5% of the total company revenues
* Started internal projects for long-lasting technical solutions, with focus to increase the company market position
